# Spotting Bias in AI Systems
*A Practical Bias Audit for Generative & Agentic AI*

Slides from Week 2 of the Women in AI Tutorials series (*Responsible & Ethical AI in Practice*), delivered with Women in AI Ireland on 13 May 2026.

## What this talk covers

A 30-minute audit anyone can run on a generative or agentic AI system. Three checks, twelve questions, designed to surface bias without requiring specialist tooling.

- **Check 1 — Who is missing?** Representation gaps in training, alignment, and retrieval.
- **Check 2 — Whose defaults?** What the system assumes when the user is vague.
- **Check 3 — Failure modes.** Where the system breaks differently for different people.

Designed for **builders, buyers, and users of AI Systems**
